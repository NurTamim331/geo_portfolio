## Visa Requirements

### SINGAPORE, MALAYSIA & THAILAND
**Common Document Checklist:**

- Passport validity: **≥ 6 months**
- 2 recent colour photographs on **matte paper**.
- **Bank Salary Statement** (6 months) **OR** Personal Bank Statement + 3 months' salary pay slip.
- Bank Solvency Certificate.
- NOC letter from office.
- Visiting card.
- National ID (NID) card.
- Office ID card copy.
- Old passport (if any).
- **For Businessmen:** Trade license with notary.
- **Housewives:** Only 2 photographs (no other documents required).
- **Students:** Student ID card / school salary books photocopy.
- **Children:** Birth certificate.

**Visa Processing Fees (BDT):**
- Malaysia: **5,500**
- Thailand: **7,000**
- Singapore: **7,000**

### CHINA – Tourist Visa
#### Personal Documents
- Current and previous passport (minimum **8 months** validity).
- Photo: **35 × 45 mm**, matte paper, white background, no cap, ears, eyes, and shoulders must be visible (hair not covering).

#### Financial Documents
- Bank statement + solvency certificate: **3 lacs** (must be signed by bank authority; e‑statements not accepted).
- Additional member: **+1 lac** per person.

#### Professional Documents (by category)
- **Job holder:** NOC, Salary Certificate, Visiting Card, ID Card.
- **Business person:** Trade License, Company pad, Visiting Card.
- **Student:** Institutional NOC, Student ID card.
- **Doctor:** BMDC Certificate, Visiting Card, NOC/Company pad.
- **Lawyer:** Bar Council Certificate, Visiting Card, NOC/Company pad.
- **Retired person:** Retirement letter or pension book.
- **Other professions:** Documents as per embassy checklist.

#### Other Documents
- TIN Certificate.
- Confirmed air ticket.
- Marriage certificate (if applicable).
- Birth certificate for children (if applicable).
- Contact number of retired and dependent persons.
- Invitation Letter (if any).
- Police GD copy (if passport lost previously).
- **Information form** – must be filled with correct details.

#### Terms & Conditions (China)
- In case of visa rejection, **embassy fee** must be paid by the applicant.
- Air ticket refund charges are borne by the applicant.
- After online approval, passport may be delivered **without** visa; all visa rights are reserved by the embassy.

#### Visa Summary
| Property          | Detail           |
|-------------------|------------------|
| **Validity**      | 90 days          |
| **Type**          | Sticker visa     |
| **Stay Permit**   | 30 days          |
| **Processing**    | 12–20 days       |
| **Fee**           | **Dynamic** – decide via call or physical meeting. |

**Application Fee (per application):** 10,000 BDT

#### Required Documents
1. Original passport.
2. TIN & Tax Certificate.
3. Bank statement – last 6 months.
4. Bank Solvency Certificate.
5. F.D.R Statement.
6. Supporting documents (if any): car, share, land, flat, etc.
7. National ID Card and Birth Certificate.
8. Visiting Card.
9. Invitation Letter + Passport copy + Tenant Agreement + Professional documents **OR** Hotel Booking.
10. Professional documents (as per applicant's occupation).
11. Marriage Certificate.
12. Vaccination Certificate.
13. Photo: 35 × 45 mm.

#### Information Required
- **Family details:** Father, Mother, Children, Sister, Brother, and Spouse – full names, dates of birth, and addresses.
- **Employment:** Service start date.
- **Education:** Last institution name, course name, and session.
